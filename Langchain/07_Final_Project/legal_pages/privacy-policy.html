Privacy Policy - WishDrop

Effective Date: 26 Feb 2026

WishDrop ("we", "our", "us") provides a WhatsApp-based price tracking and family wishlist service.

Information We Collect

  • Phone number and WhatsApp profile metadata provided by WhatsApp.
  • Messages you send to the bot (for example: product links, commands, wishlist requests).
  • Product tracking data (tracked links, target prices, price alerts).
  • Family/wishlist data you create (member names/nicknames and related wishlist items).
  • Basic technical logs (timestamps, delivery status, error logs).

How We Use Information

  • To operate the bot and process your commands.
  • To track product prices and send alerts.
  • To show your personal and family wishlist data.
  • To improve service quality, reliability, and security.

Legal Basis / Consent

By messaging our bot, you consent to processing needed to provide the service. You may opt out anytime by sending STOP.

Data Sharing

We may share data only with service providers necessary to run the product, such as Meta/WhatsApp Cloud API, hosting, database, and logging providers. We do not sell personal data.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for service operation and legal/security purposes. You can request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

  • Request access to your stored data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account data
  • Stop all messages by sending STOP

Data Deletion

Send DELETE on WhatsApp or email us at your-email@example.com. We process deletion requests within a reasonable period.

Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no system is 100% secure.

Children

This service is not intended for children under 13 without parental supervision.
