Data Deletion Instructions - WishDrop

To delete your data:

  1. Send DELETE to the WishDrop WhatsApp bot from your registered number, or
  2. Email your-email@example.com from the same registered contact details.

Please include

  • Your WhatsApp number with country code
  • The request text: "Delete my WishDrop data"

What we delete

  • Profile and contact records
  • Watchlists and family wishlist data
  • Alert history (except minimal logs required for legal/security compliance)

Estimated processing time: up to 7 business days.
